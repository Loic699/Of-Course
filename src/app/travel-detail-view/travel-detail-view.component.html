About this area Bali is one of the most exciting destinations in Southeast Asia as it is one of the first cities in the world to implement eco-friendly planning with parks, gardens and stunning trees. The city-state is also a cultural melting pot shaped by Malaysian, Chinese and Indian influences. Celebrations such as the Chinese New Year, the Muslim Eid al-Fitr celebrations in June and the Hindu Deepavali festival of lights in October attract thousands of visitors every year.
